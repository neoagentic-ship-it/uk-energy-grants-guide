# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving UK energy grants information.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fix errors** — if any grant information is out of date, please open a PR
2. **Add new schemes** — regional or local grants we've missed
3. **Improve examples** — better code samples, clearer explanations
